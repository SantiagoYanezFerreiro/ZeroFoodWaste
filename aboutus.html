What We're About

X is an online platform to raise awareness about Global Food Waste and reduce their impact in the world.

Food Waste is a serious issue that has an enormous impact on Climate Change and World Hunger.

Get in touch with us to become a partner, donating or any suggestion you have.

Also don’t forget to subscribe to our newsletter to get frequent updates and relevant information.

How We Began

We started as a small garage start-up that managed to raise 3 million € its first year.

We also won several international Social Entrepreneurship awards.

Nowadays we are looking forward to expanding our services beyond Europe as we are totally determined to eliminate Global Food Waste!
